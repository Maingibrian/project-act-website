Our Core Mandate

Health Begins
Outside the Clinic.

Revital Healthcare is dedicated to the preservation of human life through world-class medical manufacturing. True public health is inextricably linked to the ecosystems we inhabit. Environmental degradation is the root cause of countless public health crises.

The Environmental Equation

Nature ⇄ Health

Every piece of plastic removed from our shores and every water source protected from contamination translates directly to a reduction in local disease. By treating the environment, we treat the patient before they ever reach the hospital.

Zero-Capital Sustainability

Built to
Outlast Us.

A cleanup treats a symptom; infrastructure cures the disease. We upcycled 55 heavy-duty metal drums from the Revital factory floor. Welded, sanitized, and wrapped in high-visibility livery by our internal teams, these bins were installed at secure, monitored access points at zero cost to the project budget.

Project A.C.T. Phases

Targeted
Interventions.

From coastal restoration to clinical care — our operational footprint spans four distinct domains, each designed to address a critical community need.

01

Communal Safety Net

Strengthened community resilience through targeted social welfare interventions, providing essential support to vulnerable groups and fostering a culture of shared responsibility.

02

Environmental Action

Deployed coordinated strike teams to extract marine debris, ghost nets, and plastics from Kilifi's coastline — restoring ecosystems and installing permanent upcycled infrastructure.

03

Bio‑hazard Intervention

Rapid-response mobilization to neutralize a severe sanitary waste crisis — extracting 2.35 tonnes of bio-hazard and eliminating disease vectors at their source.

04

Clinical Medical Intervention

Facilitated a high-volume free eye camp in partnership with Lions International — screening 375 patients, dispensing 91 glasses, and funding 10 life-altering cataract surgeries.

Phase 06 · World Environment Day

5,000 Trees.
20 Bins.

On Friday, June 5, 2026, Project A.C.T. participated as a headline corporate partner in the official Kilifi County World Environment Day celebrations, hosted at Pwani University. The event brought together the County Government, NEMA, academia, and the private sector in a massive multi-stakeholder coalition.

"By planting 5,000 trees, we proved our willingness to do the hard, physical work of conservation. By donating 20 Legacy Bins, we proved our commitment to circular, sustainable infrastructure."

The Kilifi County Director of Environment publicly commended Project A.C.T. for its sustained, measurable impact — a milestone that cements Revital Healthcare's reputation as a premier corporate sustainability partner.

Project A.C.T. Phase 05 delivered a free eye care camp at Kikambala, partnering with licensed optometrists to screen, diagnose, and treat community members with zero cost to participants.
