5,000 Trees
for the Future

On June 5, 2026, Revital Healthcare participated as a headline corporate partner in the official Kilifi County World Environment Day celebrations at Pwani University. Volunteers equipped with jembes deployed to the Forest Farm and planted 5,000 indigenous and shade trees alongside students and community members.

Infrastructure

20 Legacy Bins
Handed Over

The climax of the corporate engagement was the official unveiling of 20 Legacy Waste Bins. Upcycled from 200L metal drums, fabricated in‑house, branded, and donated to Pwani University, Kilifi Municipality, and Mtwapa Municipality — a permanent solution to localized littering.

Recognition

Public
Commendation

During the official speeches, the Kilifi County Director of Environment publicly commended Project A.C.T. for its sustained, measurable impact over the past six months — validating the project's strategy and solidifying Revital Healthcare's reputation as a premier corporate sustainability partner.
