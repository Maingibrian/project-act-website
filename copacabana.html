05 · Qualitative Impact

A Shore
Restored.

40 Sacks — Marine Debris Extracted

PET plastics, ghost fishing gear, waterlogged tires, and sand bags removed from the tidal zone. Every sector of the targeted beach was aggressively cleared to the micro-level.

21 Legacy Bins — Permanent Infrastructure

200‑litre industrial drums — upcycled directly from Revital's factory floor, sanitized, branded, and permanently anchored at primary access points at near‑zero capital cost.

0% Leakage — Full Accountability

KES 36,000 cash deployed. Every shilling accounted for. Bins and PPE sourced internally. Zero waste abandonment — the chain‑of‑custody remained entirely unbroken.

Inter‑Organizational Blueprint

Phase 02 proved A.C.T.'s capacity to manage complex environmental interventions. The operation cemented long-term trust between Revital Healthcare and the local government.
